Incept AI

Building AI driven expert systems that brings personalized help to the masses

About Us

Our mission is to build expert systems that brings personalized help to the masses. We want to use the recent growth in AI to build a rule based expert system that learns from human experts and customer data in real time to solve problems in a scalable way.

As a first step in this direction, we have built a couple of networking tools that use our expertise in wireless networks to solve problems related to slow wifi around the world. Our first solutions -- Wifi Tester and Wifi Expert -- gather data about each potential customer from their phone, then uses a set of rules to come up with the right solution for their problem. Human experts get involved when the system sees a new problem. Over time our system also learns new rules and requires less human involvement.
